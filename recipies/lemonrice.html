Lemon Rice Recipie

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Rice
  • asafatodia
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 cup curry leaves
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder(haldi)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves chopped
  • 2 tbsp peanuts
  • 1 tsp channa dal
  • 1 piece ginger
  • 2 red chilly

Steps

  1. Put Your rice in cooker, clean it and soak it for 10 mins
  2. Pour water in 1:2 ratio and place the lid on cooker and let it cook for 2 whistles
  3. In a tawa, add 2 tsp oil, mustard seeds, asafatodia,ginger,channa dal, curry leaves and saute them well
  4. When the seeds begin to splutter, add peanuts, turmeric powder followed by rice. Turn around a few times so the rice is completely mixed.
  5. Add salt and lemon juice to the rice and mix well.
  6. Voila, Lemon rice is now ready!
